I did my industrial attachment or internship in Skymech Automation & Engineering. Skymech specialises in designing customized products using automated machines and robotic integration. During my time there, I mainly did 3D CAD designs and 2D part drawings.

One of my main projects during my time in Skymech is to design a portion of a large Packaging and Assembly Machine. The designs made in Skymech are confidential and not much images can be shared but I managed to get approval on some of them. The part that I designed for the Packaging and Assembly Machine consist of various components such as the Labelling Machine, Taping Machine, Conveyor Belts and a Universal Robot.
